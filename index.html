Corporate Debt, Distress and More Borrowing

A bubble chart timeline showing the turbulance in the corporate bond market at the start of pandemic in the U.S. Working with the reporter, we found data on "new-issue concession," or extra yield, investors demanded during this period. I highlighed companies with notable bond sales as well as the dearth of new bonds before the Federal Reserve stepped in to stabilize the market.

Wall Street Journal, May 2020

How the Bull Market Unraveled

Using scroll to control the a heatmap timeline, we highlightd important dates and events that led to the end of the longest-ever bull market on March 19, 2020. While the market would recover, this captures a point in time when experts still thought the financial future of the country was precarious. I worked with two other graphics reporters to design, build and report this graphic.

Wall Street Journal, March 2020
